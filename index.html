Few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the sun and is the only known planet to support life, featuring 70% water-covered surface and a protective atmosphere. It is an "oblate spheroid," not a perfect sphere, and has a stable tilt that creates seasons. The Earth is ~4.54 billion years old, rotates on its axis causing day and night, orbits the sun causing years, and has a permanent natural satellite, the Moon, which helps stabilize its tilt and influences tides.
